performer desk

The performer desk can be positioned in the centre of the room facing the projection screen (placed on the stage), or he can be directly on the stage, facing the audience.

A 200x100x80 centimeters desk is required.


audio equipment

An audio system arranged according to standard criteria is required (with the speakers directly adjacent to the left and right sides of the projection screen) with the following characteristics:

  • high quality P.A. line array system, correctly dimensioned and aligned (reference brands: MEYER SOUND, D&B AUDIOTECHNIK, L- ACOUSTIC, MARTIN;
  • the sound coverage of the entire listening area must be guaranteed with a distortion-free 110dB SPL sound pressure level and with a response in frequency as linear as possible;
  • subwoofers correctly dimensioned.

A 24 channel analog mixer, positioned on the performer desk, is required (such as Allen & Heath ZED-428).

A stereo monitoring is required if the performer is placed on the stage.


video projection

A projection system with the following characteristics is required:

  • projector, minimum 5000 ANSI lumens with wide optics (0.8);
  • projection screen (minimum 5 meters wide) on the stage;
  • HDMI connection;
  • the projection can be frontal or rear.


soundcheck

2 hours for setup and sound-check are required.


lighting

The show is in the total dark. It is therefore required that the main room is completely darkened (even the emergency lights must be kept to the minimum).

A lighting for the performer is required during the applause.
